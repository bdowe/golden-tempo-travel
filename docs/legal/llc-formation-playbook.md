## Future gates

- **H2 2026 — domicile decision** before going nomadic in 2027: keep NJ
  (taxes worldwide income; sticky domicile) vs. establish SD/FL/TX first.
  If moving: domesticate the LLC out under NJ RULLCA (same EIN/bank; SD, FL,
  TX, WY all accept inbound). Also swap registered agent to a commercial one
  (~$125/yr) + virtual mailbox before leaving.
- **Before Phase 2 ships (in-app flight booking, Duffel merchant model)** —
  the company becomes a seller of travel: Seller of Travel registration
  (CA/FL/WA/HI; CA has bond/trust rules), E&O insurance, payment processing
  under the LLC, confirm Duffel merchant-of-record split of obligations.
  **Lawyer required here — the one milestone where DIY stops.**
- **Before Phase 3 ships (paid tier)** — SaaS sales tax: Stripe Tax or a
  merchant-of-record (Paddle/Lemon Squeezy); refund policy in ToS.
- **At ~$50k+/yr consistent profit** — revisit S-corp election with a CPA
  (below that, payroll costs eat the savings; interacts badly with the
  foreign earned income exclusion while abroad 330+ days).
